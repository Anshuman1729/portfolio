How I Think

Principles

01 —

Predictability over volatility

A channel that delivers 80% of target every month beats one that swings 50–150%. Compounding needs a stable foundation before it needs a higher ceiling.

02 —

Segment before you scale

Scaling on blended averages is a trap. Know exactly which cohorts carry the numbers before you pour fuel — otherwise you're growing your worst customers fastest.

03 —

Economics-first growth

ROAS and CAC aren't just reporting metrics — they're the guardrails that determine whether growth is real or accounting fiction. Optimize for the former, report on the latter.

04 —

Structure beats hacks

Creative fatigue, audience overlap, bid cannibalization — most performance problems are structural, not tactical. Fix the system, not the symptom, and the numbers follow.
